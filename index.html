About Us

Serving Since 2020

The Classic Brew started with a simple idea: to create a place where coffee lovers could enjoy handcrafted brews in a warm, welcoming space. Nestled in the heart of Cavite, our shop blends tradition and quality, sourcing only the finest beans from around the world to bring out rich, bold flavors in every cup.

Whether you're stopping by for your daily dose of caffeine or looking for a quiet spot to unwind, The Classic Brew is more than just a coffee shop—it's a place where every cup is made with care, and every visit feels like home.
